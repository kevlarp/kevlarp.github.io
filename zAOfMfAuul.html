Wedding Schedule

Friday, April 17th, 2020

2 - 4 p.m.

Hike in Almaden Quicksilver County Park

If you're in town and are so inclined, join the bride and groom for a hike in Almaden Quicksilver State Park. We'll meet at the Mockingbird Hill entrance, where there is a large parking lot. We will plan for a 4-mile hike but can adjust plans on the fly.

12 - 5 p.m.

Ceremony and reception

Sunday, April 19th, 2020

11 a.m. - 2 p.m.

Farewell brunch

The bride and groom will be hosting a light brunch (i.e. bagels etc.) at their home at 171 Monroe St. in Santa Clara, CA. There is free street parking.
