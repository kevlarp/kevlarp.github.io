Ground Transportation

You will need a car to get to Sanborn County Park. The park will charge an entrance fee of $6 per vehicle. Proceed to Sequoia/Peterson Grove Picnic Area for parking.

Public transit is generally the best way to get around in San Francisco and the East Bay (MUNI, BART, AC Transit). This is not the case in San Jose, where your best bet is probably to rent a car or take Uber/Lyft. The public transit options that exist include the VTA, which operates a light rail and bus system, and Caltrain.
